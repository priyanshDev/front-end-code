WORKOUT ROUTINE FOR WOMEN

This workout routine for women typically involves a Brisk walking or jogging, cycling, swimming, yoga, cardiovascular exercise, and core strengthening, Flexibility training along with warm-up and cool-down exercises. To create an effective routine, women can focus on their specific fitness goals, such as building muscle, losing weight or improving endurance.
